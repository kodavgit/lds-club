About Us

Who We Are

The Ennyston College Literary and Debating Society (LDS) is an extracurricular society dedicated to nurturing eloquent speakers, confident performers, thoughtful writers and future leaders. Through debates, poetry, drama, public speaking and creative writing, members develop critical thinking, communication, confidence and teamwork skills that prepare them for success both inside and outside the classroom.

LDS Anthem: We Are Players

🎵

Chorus

We are players, not spectators, hear our call!
To impact, impress and to win over all.
With courage and integrity, we take our stand,
The Literary and Debating Society of this land!

From the halls of Ennyston, a voice rings clear,
For the thinkers, the writers, dispelling fear.
With words as our weapon and truth as our light,
We stand in the arena, prepared for the fight.

From poetry's rhythm to debate's sharp clash,
To the stage's great drama, the writer's flash.
We delve in the questions the wide world imparts,
With open minds and with compassionate hearts.

For the school we uphold, for the future we seek,
With wisdom and passion, both strong and unique.
We pledge to this fellowship, to learn and to lead,
By our founding motto, in thought and in deed.
